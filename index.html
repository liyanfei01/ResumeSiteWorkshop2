ABOUT ME

I was born in China, I grew up in Brazil and I live in Chicago for over 15 years. I'm a user-centered designer, focused on creating strategic solutions with the purpose of helping eliminate product and services pain-points.  This admiration for user experience branched from an architecture background, which taught me the core principles of designing for the client. From here, my empathetic personality and critical thinking drove me to the UXUI design immersive course at Ironhack SP. The 10-week Bootcamp helped refine my skills further as well as fueling my ambition to search for a UX Design role. ​ In my next role, I want to be involved in user experience design for the retail & hospitality industry, in a multidisciplinary team, remote or flexible.
